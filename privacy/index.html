Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how RagnarOak ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when you visit ragnaroak.us or purchase our products.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when placing an order or contacting us, including your name, billing and shipping address, email address, phone number, and payment information. Payment details are processed securely by our retailer, BuyGoods, and are not stored on our servers. We also automatically collect limited technical data such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited, using cookies and similar technologies.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process and fulfill your orders, provide customer support, send order confirmations and shipping updates, respond to inquiries, improve our website and products, and, where you have opted in, send promotional communications. You may opt out of marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link.

How We Share Your Information

We share information only as needed to operate our business: with our retailer BuyGoods to process payments, with shipping and fulfillment partners to deliver your order, and with service providers who support our website and customer service. We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to remember your preferences, analyze traffic, and support marketing. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function properly as a result.

Data Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@healthyconsumer.us.

Children's Privacy

Our products and website are intended for adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.
